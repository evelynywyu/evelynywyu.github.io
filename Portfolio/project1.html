About the project

In the class "Introduction of Interaction Design", we followed the design principle and designed a solution for a research problem that we are passionate about. I am passionate about sports. I have seen a lot of my friends get injuried during exercise but keep exercising, which is not the purpose of exercise. Hence, I want to create an app that can help people monitor their exercise and provide them with personalized exercise advice.
